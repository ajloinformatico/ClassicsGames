Classics Games Version 1.1

Games

Connect 4

Connect 4 tiles that have the same color before your opponent.
Connect all the tiles you can diagonally or vertically

Memory Game

is a simple single player game where we have to flip two cards to find matches.
If you find the whole match you win.

Whack a Mole

is a simple game where you will have to click on the more before it changes position.
Get all the points you can by clicking on the mole

Snake Game

You are in control of a snake as it crawls and collects apples.
The more apples you eat, the harder it will be.

Space Invaders

Space invaders Galaxy Retro is a fun shooting game for kids and adults.
Our planet is under attack and you need to defend it. Have fun playing Space Invaders

Frogger Game

The objective of the game is to guide a frog to the top of the screen.
But be careful you will have to jump avoiding the traps
